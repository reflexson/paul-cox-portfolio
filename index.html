About Me

Paul has had a passion for computers since the 1980s when he first started using msdos to run code. He has designed and built several recording studios in his 20 years in the recording industry. Paul's attention now focusses on software development. He uses the skills he has acquired from building computers, working in teams, and intense coding training to make a formidable developer. Paul's attention to detail and passion for making things execute flawlessly makes him an ideal candidate for any development team with proficiencies in HTML, CSS, JS, Python, and C++.
